Reynard Verill Buntoro

First of all, welcome to my website and nice to meet you! I am a computer science sophomore at HKU with an intended minor of finance. My interest ranges from artificial intelligence up to drawing digital arts using my IPad with the help of procreate. I am an enthusiastic learner, especially about new things which I find intriguing within the realm of computer science as they never fail to astonish me. The course not only help me understand the core principals and fundamentals of the respected topics, but also nurture me to engage in assorted hands-on projects. If you are interested about the projects that I have done in the past (both independent and school-related projects), do not hesitate to contact me directly or check-out my github (I usually only upload the independent ones to github).
